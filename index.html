Rules

To win a round of Minesweeper, you must click on the board every square that doesn't have a mine under it. Once you've done so, the game will be over. If you accidentally click a square that has a mine beneath it, the game will be over. You can start over and play again!

How to play ?

Left mouse click to uncover a tile. Right click allow to put a flag on the covered tiles, where you think the mine is. Double left click or right click on the uncovered number, allow to uncover tiles around where shouldn't be any mines, if you put correct numbers of flags around the tile with number. Good luck!
